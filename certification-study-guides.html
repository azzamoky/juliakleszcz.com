Certification Study Guides

Prepare for your Salesforce certification with these comprehensive study guides...

Administrator Certification

Administrator Certification

Learn the essential skills and knowledge required to pass the Salesforce Administrator Certification exam. The following topics are crucial:

  • User Management
  • Security and Access
  • Standard and Custom Objects
  • Sales and Marketing Applications
  • Service and Support Applications
  • Activity Management and Collaboration
  • Data Management
  • Analytics - Reports and Dashboards
  • Workflow/Process Automation

Developer Certification

Master the basics of programming on the Salesforce platform with a focus on:

  • Apex Programming
  • Visualforce Pages
  • Lightning Components
  • Salesforce Integration
  • Testing and Debugging

Advanced Certifications

For those looking to further their expertise, explore advanced certifications like:

  • Salesforce Architect
  • Salesforce Consultant
  • Salesforce Marketing Cloud
  • Salesforce Pardot
