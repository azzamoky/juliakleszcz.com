About Me

Hi, I'm Julia Kleszcz, a passionate and experienced Salesforce professional dedicated to helping businesses optimize their use of the Salesforce platform. With years of experience as a Salesforce Administrator and Developer, I have developed a deep understanding of Salesforce's capabilities and best practices.

I specialize in user management, security, automation, and custom development, ensuring that Salesforce solutions are tailored to meet the unique needs of each organization. My approach is both strategic and hands-on, focusing on achieving tangible results and empowering users to leverage Salesforce effectively.

As a mentor and instructor, I take pride in teaching others how to navigate and excel in the Salesforce ecosystem. I provide comprehensive training sessions, create detailed documentation, and offer personalized support to ensure that my clients and students succeed.

My commitment to continuous learning and professional development keeps me at the forefront of Salesforce advancements, enabling me to provide cutting-edge solutions and insights. I am always eager to share my knowledge and collaborate with others to drive innovation and excellence.

Whether you're looking to implement Salesforce, improve your existing setup, or prepare for certification, I'm here to help. Let's work together to unlock the full potential of Salesforce for your business.
